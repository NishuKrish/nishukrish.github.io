Nishaanth Krishnan

College Admissions Counseling

About

Nishaanth Krishnan is a senior at UCLA majoring in Statistics and Data Science. He is passionate about helping students get into their dream colleges.

During his undergraduate career, Nishaanth has completed internships and apprenticeships at companies like Meta and Netflix. He has also served as a peer college advisor at the UCLA Office of Admissions

Nishaanth's work has helped get students into:

Yale, University of Chicago, UC Berkeley, UCLA, University of Michigan, New York University, Boston College, and University of Washington among others.

Work samples are available upon request.
